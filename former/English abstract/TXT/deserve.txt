deserve:应得、应受、值得
~ sth  ~ our praise   ~ the punishment 

sth ~ to be done 应该被做

The problem deserves to be solved 

1、他为人类的繁荣进步作出巨大贡献，应该得到最高荣誉
He has made great contributions to human prosperity and deserves the highest honor

2、任何帮助残疾人的事业应该得到全社会的关心和支持
Any cause to help the disabled deserves concern and support from the whole society
to help the disabled:做cause的定语  the + adj 表示一类人

3、虽然默默无闻，他们应该得到全社会的尊敬和关爱
Although (they are )unknown to the public . they deserve respect and caring hearts from whole society

4、我认为他很聪明，有创造性，应该得到这个奖
To my mind, he is very talented with creative power and deserves the award

5、爸爸在我们的心中拥有特别的地位，在父亲节这天他应该得到我们最好的祝福
Father holds a special place in our hearts. on Father's Day, he deserves the best

有Day的节日前面不加the

6、让我们携起手来，创造一个和谐的社会，在其中每个人应该得到尊敬，尊严(dignity)和友善
Let's join hands to build a harmonious society where everyone deserves respect,dignity and warmth

where :在其中，所以为定语从句
