1、ablity: 能力  disablity：残疾  disabled：残疾的

response  n            对……反 应
                to sth   
respond   vt           对……作出反应

responsibility  有责任心 

对……负责
take the responsibility of sth  ==be responsible for sth  

2、to one's greatest ability  尽某人最大努力做某事

you should understand your supportive mother to your greatest ability 
在长辈前加上supportive 修饰时表示尊敬的意思
另外your greatest ability 中 当最高级前被定语修饰时不加the 例如：my best friend 


例句：
1.全世界的人应该精诚合作尽最大努力保护我们脆弱的生态环境
People worldwide should join hands to protect our weak eco-system to our greatest ability 

join hands to do sth ：合作做某事     joint：adj 共同的，联合的;?

2.我们应该尽最大努力解决这个复杂的难题 
We should solve the challenging problem to our greatest ability 

challenging ：adj 挑战性的;?引起兴趣的;?令人深思的

3.他尽最大努力完成了这项艰巨的任务
He fulfilled the challenging task to his greatest ability  (已完成,过去式)                                     以L结尾的动词，现在分词和过去分词一般都要写成双写L，再加上ed 例如 fulfil--->fulfilled
  
4.他抓住了难得的机会展现了自己的才能赢得了我们的掌声   
He grasped/seized the precious chance to display/present his ability and won our clapping 
grasped/seized 是过去时所以and 后的win 要变成 won 保持时态一致

5.他们定期举行各种各样的英语活动，提高他们的英语口语水平
They hold English activities of all descriptions regularly to level up their ability of spoken English 
要学会使用of短语 their spoken English ability --->their ability of spoken English 

written English   of all descriptions  ： 各种各样的 一定要加s，因为各种各样表示很多

6.我们应该尽最大努力帮他们走出困境
We should help them get out of the awkward position to our greatest ability 
get out of: 走出
