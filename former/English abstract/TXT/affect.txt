1、他把一生都献给革命事业，他的精神感动了世界各地无数的青年人
He devoted all his life to revolution cause and his spirits affected countless teenagers worldwide


2、意外的飓风，袭击了城市严重地影响了当地人的生活
The accidental hurricane hit/strike the city , affecting the life of local people severely


3、我们应该理性的对待这个问题，将副作用减少到最低
We should treat the problem reasonably and reduce the side effects to the minimum / maximum

treat / take sb/sth + adv 理性对待问题   take sb well / badly 对……怎么样

4、调查表明了室内环境成为影响人们健康的四个主要因素之一，40%的疾病有室内的污染引起的
The reserach indicates that indoor has become one of the four principal factors affecting people's health and 40% of the diseases are caused by indoor pollution 

principal :adj 重要的，主要的，资本的，本金的 n：首长，主角，负责人

5、校园网是对外宣传和交流的窗口，对学校发展各方面起着很大作用
Campus network, a window of outside communication and publicity , has great effect on all aspects in the development of schools

publicity :宣传，公共信息，宣传效用
