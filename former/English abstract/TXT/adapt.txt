1、adapt to sth: 适应
2、adapt：~ a novel :改编
3、adaptable: 能适应的，可改编的 an adaptable person
   adjust to sth:适应  adjust：调整  adjust time
4、make an adjustment,physically and mentally

adopt: 收养，采取，采纳

例句：
1、你应该学会独立，以适应大学生活
You should learn to be independent to adapt to the life of college 

learn to be +adj

2、你最好身心调整，以适应新学期繁忙的生活
You'd better make an adjustment, physically and mentally,to adapt to the busy life of the brand-new term
physically and mentally :作后置定语

3、他有很强的团队精神，善于沟通能很快适应各种各样的环境
He has strong team spirits with good communication skills and can adapt to environment of all descriptions quickly

4、大学生应不断调整心理状态，以适应竞争激烈的环境
College students should adjust their mindstate constantly to adapt to the environment of fierce competition
constant: adj 不断的 instant：瞬间的，速溶的

5、信息时代要求人们敢于承担责任，独立思考，知道怎么学习，知道瞬息万变的世界
Information age requires that we should dare to take the responsibility,think independently and know how to study in order to adapt to the rapidly changing world 


6、年复一年，我们都学会了调整自己，学会了彼此宽容，学会了适应各种不同的环境
Over the years,we have learned to adjust ,to forgive each other and to adapt to environment of all descriptions
