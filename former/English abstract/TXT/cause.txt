cause:n 原因(不好的方面) 事业  cause and effect：因果关系
vt：
    cause sth: 引起某事
          sb to do sth …引起某人做某事   The rain caused him to be late

throw away ：扔掉    throw …to sb 把…扔给…

1、人们到处仍塑料袋，严重污染了环境
People throw away plastic bags whenever and wherever, causing serious environmental pollution

2、许多学生过度用眼，带来了近视的问题
Many students overuse their eyes, causing the problem of short-sightedness
