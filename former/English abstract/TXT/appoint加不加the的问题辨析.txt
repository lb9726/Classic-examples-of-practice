appoint:任命
1、~ sb as……任命
2、~ a date / a place 约定
3、appointed time / place 约定
4、appointment

1、在约定的时间，他没有来，这使得在场的人很生气
He didn't turn up at the appointed time ,which annoyed the people 'present


2、怀特有想象力，有创造性，任命为俱乐部主席
White,described as being creative and imaginative ,was appointed as chairman of the club 

described as being:可以省略   另外：chairman 前不能加the 

3、我们约定一个时间面谈好吗？我的号码是
Shall we appoint a time to make an interview ? I can be reached at ……


4、我很高兴的向你们推荐玛丽小姐，我想如果任命的话，她会表现极为出色
I recommend you miss Mary with pleasure and have the confidence that if(she is) appointed,she will display her  shining abilities 

5、我有责任感，在校担任班长，每天打扫卫生我总是最后一个离开教室
I am a person with a sense of responsibility . Appointed as monitor , I am always the last one to leave the classroom when  doing cleaning every day

doing：表示主动  
leave…for 离开…去某地
leave vt leave the school

6、因为发生了意外的事，我不知道我们是否把我们的约定从星期一换成星期三
Owing to something unexpected , I wonder if we could change our appointment from Monday to Wednesday

wonder :想知道  wonderful：精彩的，令人高兴的
Owing to + n 因为
