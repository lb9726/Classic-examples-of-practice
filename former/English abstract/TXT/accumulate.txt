accumulate : 积累

1、他们努力的工作着，积累了许多财富
They stayed dedicated to their work and accumulated a world of wealth
许多： a world of  a sea of a host of 

2、我希望你积极参加各种各样的课外活动，积累足够的社会经验
I hope you will be a positive goer in after-class activities of all descripitions to accumulate enough social experience 
此处使用hope不使用wish 因为wish 是不能成功的事，hope 的从句一定要使用将来时
例如：I hope you will succeed 

3、我们应该积累新的经验当新问题出现时，提出新的解决办法
we should accumulate new exprience and try new solutions when new problems arise 
arise arouse arisen

4、为了提高他的语感，他采取了一些策略，提高他的听力，积累了足够的语言和文化知识
To improve his sense of language, he adopted some strategies to level up his listening and accumulated enough knowledge on language and cultrue
adopted 采取，采纳   后面的accumulated 要与adopted 时态一致

5、他积累了相关的工作经验拥有了很好的交流、协调、组织能力
He accumulated related work exprience and possessed good ability of communication，harmonization and organization 
harmony n:和谐   harmonious adj 和谐的
possessed 拥有

6、我从来没有听说过谁没有经过努力就出类拔萃或者积累了丰富的知识
I have never heard /know any person to distinguish himself and accumulate good knowledge without efforts

heard 过去分词加d   never 常常和完成时搭配   
distinguish 区别  ~ A and B   distinction 区别
distinguished ：尊贵的
