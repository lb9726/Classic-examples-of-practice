add :vt 又说，又问
2、add to 增加  ~ to our confidence 
3、add …(前)to…(后)  给后者加前者
add sugar to the water
4、add up to 总计、达到，总共
5、in addition = additionally 此外

1、他说每个公民应该负起责任保护我们珍贵的自然资源
He added that every citizen should take the responsibility of the pretection of our precious natural resources


2、他在比赛中获得了第一名，这增加了他的自信心
He took the first place in the competition ,which added to his confidence to succeed

3、此外在人际交往中学会微笑，因为微笑能帮你走出困境
In addition,we should learn to smile in human communication . You know ,smiling can open doors and tear down walls


4、此外，我们为你们自强不息的努力和挑战自我的精神所感动
In addition,we are deeply moved with your efforts to reach for a higher goal and spirits to challenge yourself


5、为了实现父母的期望而产生的压力增加了现代年轻人的危机感
The pressure to live up to parents' expectation  adds to the sense of crisis among young adults

to live up to parents' expectation: 做The pressure 的定语
所以 adds 是单三，主语为：The pressure

6、他补充道，你可能是个合格的候选人，但如果面试时不自信，那一切就都不重要了
"You may be a qualified candidate but none of that matters if you walk into an interview lacking confidence " he added.
matters:用于疑问句否定句中，不用于肯定句中
