1、acquire = obtain = attain = get 获得

acqusition ：n 得到，习得
everyday：adj 日常的  every day adv 每天
例句：
1.他英语很好能胜任这个工作
He acquired a high level of fluency and accuracy in English and could be equal to the challenging work

a high level of fluency and accuracy in English  ：表示什么很好的一种高级表达方式
could 不能写成can 因为要与acquired 的时态保持一致
fluent adj 流利的，流畅的 accurate  adj 准确的，精准的

2.你应该积极参加社会活动获得足够的生活经验使自己成熟
You should be a positive goer in social activities to acquire enough life experience and mature yourself 
          adj  成熟的，仔细考虑的
mature                               maturity：n 成熟
          vt  使成熟


3.我认为如果你想获得他的理解，你最好先道歉
To my mind，if you have a hunger to acquire her understanding ，you should make an apology to her first
apologize to sb for sth :为某事向某人道歉
make an apology to sb for sth :为某事向某人道歉  an apology  元音字母a 开头冠词为an a是错误的
  
4.在英语学习过程中，我们应该有个好的英语环境，每天练习说英语
In English acquisition，we should be surrounded by it and practise speaking it every day 
practice= habbit n.练习，实践，行为，习惯
practise v practise doing sth
